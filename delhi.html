Delhi & Taj Mahal

5 Days, 4 Nights

Full Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in the Capital

Fly from Kannur to New Delhi. Upon arrival, you'll be transferred to a premium hotel in the city center. Rest and acclimate, then enjoy an evening walk around Connaught Place.

Day 2: Heritage of Delhi

A full-day guided tour of Delhi. We start with the imposing Red Fort, followed by a rickshaw ride through the bustling streets of Chandni Chowk. Later, visit India Gate, the Lotus Temple, and the towering Qutub Minar.

Day 3: Journey to Agra

After breakfast, travel to Agra via the Yamuna Expressway. Check in at your hotel. In the evening, visit Mehtab Bagh for a mesmerizing sunset view of the Taj Mahal from across the river.

Day 4: The Symbol of Love

Early morning visit to the Taj Mahal at sunrise - a truly magical experience. Return to the hotel for breakfast. Later, explore the massive Agra Fort and the abandoned city of Fatehpur Sikri.

Day 5: Departure

Drive back to Delhi International Airport for your return flight to Kannur. Carry memories of India's golden heritage.
