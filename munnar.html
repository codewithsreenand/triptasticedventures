Munnar & Ooty Escape

4 Days, 3 Nights

Full Itinerary

Day 1: Departure & Arrival

Depart from Kannur/Kasaragod in our luxurious, fully air-conditioned Komban Bus. Enjoy the scenic drive through the Western Ghats. Arrive in Munnar, check in to your premium resort, and spend the evening enjoying a guided walk through the lush green tea plantations.

Day 2: Exploring Munnar

After breakfast, visit the famous Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. In the afternoon, head to Mattupetty Dam for a speed boat ride. We depart for Ooty in the evening.

Day 3: The Queen of Hills - Ooty

Wake up to the chill breeze of Ooty. Visit the mesmerizing Botanical Gardens and Doddabetta Peak. In the afternoon, experience the heritage Nilgiri Mountain Railway (Toy Train) ride.

Day 4: Boating & Departure

Morning boating experience at Ooty Lake. After a local shopping tour for homemade chocolates and eucalyptus oil, we begin our comfortable return journey via Komban Bus back to Kannur/Kasaragod.
