Rajasthan Royals

6 Days, 5 Nights

Full Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

Board your flight from Kannur International Airport to Jaipur. Upon arrival, check into a heritage hotel. Spend the evening walking through the vibrant local bazaars of the Pink City.

Day 2: The Pink City Tour

Visit the majestic Amber Fort and enjoy an elephant ride. Stop by the iconic Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds) and the City Palace. Experience authentic Rajasthani cuisine at night.

Day 3: Journey to Jodhpur

Travel to the Blue City, Jodhpur. Check in to your hotel and visit the colossal Mehrangarh Fort, which offers a stunning panoramic view of the blue-painted houses below.

Day 4: Jaisalmer & The Thar Desert

Head to the Golden City, Jaisalmer. In the afternoon, transition to a desert camp. Enjoy a thrilling camel safari across the Sam Sand Dunes, followed by a folk dance and music performance around a bonfire.

Day 5: Jaisalmer Fort

Explore the living Jaisalmer Fort, one of the very few forts in the world where people still reside. Visit Patwon Ki Haveli and Gadisar Lake.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight back to Kannur. Take home royal memories of Rajasthan.
